Cheesesteak

Chopped up steak with sauteed onions and veggies of choice. Mixed with either white or yellow American cheese.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye steak
  • Onions
  • American cheese

Steps

  1. Saute onions until translucent
  2. Thinly slice ribeye strips
  3. Once onions are done, cook steak on medium heat without browning. Salt and pepper to taste
  4. When steak is done, add in cheese and onions to combine all flavors

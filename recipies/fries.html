Fries

Deep fried potatoe slices salted, served fresh and hot.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Slice up potatoes to your desired thickness.
  2. Heat up vegetable oil to 350 degrees farenheight.
  3. Once temp is reach gently drop in fries for 5-7 minutes.
  4. When fries are fully cooked immediately salt and pepper.

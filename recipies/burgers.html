Burgers

Meat patty inside of a brioche bun with toppings of your choice.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Brioche Buns
  • Salt Pepper and Condiments

Steps

  1. Toast bun
  2. Shape ground beef into a ball.
  3. Turn on heat to the max.
  4. When ready smash burger on skillet for 3 minutes per side
  5. Salt and pepper both sides
  6. Add cheese 1 minute before removing patty from the heat
